Omlette

Description

A simple recipe on how to make a delicious Omlette

Ingredients

  • 2-3 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon olive oil
  • 1/4 small onion, finely diced
  • 1/4 cup chopped tomato
  • 1/2 cup fresh spinach
  • Salt and black pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Saut√© onion, tomato, and spinach in olive oil until softened; set aside.
  2. Whisk eggs with salt and pepper.
  3. Pour eggs into the hot pan, cooking until edges set.
  4. Sprinkle cooked vegetables over one half; fold the other half over.
  5. Cook until done, then serve immediately.
