Carbonara

Description

A simple recipe on how to make a delicious Carbonara

Ingredients

  • 200g spaghetti or bucatini
  • 100g guanciale (or pancetta), diced
  • 2 large egg yolks
  • 50g Pecorino Romano cheese, grated
  • Black pepper, freshly cracked

Steps

  1. Boil pasta in salted water until al dente.
  2. While pasta cooks, render diced guanciale in a dry pan until crispy.
  3. In a bowl, whisk egg yolks, grated Pecorino, and plenty of black pepper.
  4. Drain pasta, reserving some pasta water. Add hot pasta directly to the pan with guanciale. Immediately stir in the egg mixture, adding a splash of reserved pasta water as needed to create a creamy sauce.
  5. Toss well until coated. Serve immediately with extra Pecorino and black pepper.
